EXHIBITIONS

ROBOTICS & AI

The information revolution brings AI and robotics to the forefront.

Embrace the future with AI's home uses and robotics in industry. Meet and greet Rob the Robot.

COSMOLOGY

Discover the universe with 'The Sky Above Us' exhibit.

Gaze at constellations and galaxies, and delve into how ancient navigators used the stars for exploration.

EVOLUTION

Explore life's origins and evolution since Darwin's groundbreaking theory.

Uncover how today's species evolved and meet extinct creatures like the Woolly Mammoth and dinosaurs.

ECOLOGY

Discover the tiny world of biology, from micro-organisms to the human body.

Explore the history of medicine, its breakthroughs like penicillin and heart transplants, and how observing animals has fueled major discoveries.

BIOLOGY & MEDICINE

Explore biology from microorganisms to human anatomy.

Discover medical milestones like penicillin and heart transplants, and learn how nature inspires medicinal breakthroughs.

SPECIAL EVENTS

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum.

It's a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them.

From solar energy powering lighting, to 'Wind Island' that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting 'space'.
